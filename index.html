Our Story

Where roots grow deep
and futures take shape

“The joy of life comes with the acceptance and pride of our roots.”

Founded in 1999, Peques has spent 27 years building a community where children from every background feel genuinely valued. Our trilingual, child-led approach is grounded in the belief that when children are nurtured with warmth, consistency, and cultural richness, they thrive in every sense.

Our Approach

Four pillars of exceptional early years

Everything we do is shaped by a holistic philosophy — nurturing the whole child, not simply school-readiness.

🌐

Trilingual Education

English, Spanish, and Mandarin woven naturally into every day. Children absorb languages effortlessly at this age — we make the most of that remarkable window of opportunity.

🧠

Holistic Development

We support the whole child: emotional, physical, and cognitive. Our child-led model follows each child’s individual pace, guided by a highly qualified and caring team.

🌿

Natural Environment

Natural materials, recycled resources, and a genuine focus on the outdoors. Imagination, curiosity, and creativity flourish in spaces designed to feel warm and like home.

🥗

Nutrition & Wellbeing

Our in-house chef and 5-star kitchen provide fresh, balanced meals throughout the day. We take nutrition as seriously as learning — because the two are inseparable.

Recognition

Trusted by families. Recognised by institutions.

Our accreditations reflect a sustained commitment to the highest standards in child safety, health, and wellbeing.

✓
Millie’s Mark

100% of our practitioners hold current paediatric first aid certification — well beyond the statutory minimum. Millie’s Mark recognises settings where children’s safety sits at the forefront of every decision.

🏅
Healthy Early Years London

Funded by the Mayor of London, HEYL recognises our commitment to child health and wellbeing across healthy eating, oral health, physical activity, and early cognitive development.

🏢
London Healthy Workplace Charter

Awarded by the Mayor of London for our commitment to a healthier, happier workplace. The wellbeing of our team is directly and inseparably connected to the quality of care every child receives.
